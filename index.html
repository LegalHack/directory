Welcome to the Legal Hackers Prototype Directory of Law & Technology Educational Programs!

This is a project to create a distributed and shared public directory service listing events, programs and other resources relevant to legal hacking. The initial prototype is focused on law & technology educational program.

To see the current listing of law and technology educational programs, see the page.

To make contributions to the directory, including proposing a new candidate programs for addition to the directory or letting us know about possible errors or other suggested improvements to the data or display, please use the inputs page.
